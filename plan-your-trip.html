Plan Your Trip

Everything you need to know for a seamless visit to Doha for QELE 2026.

Event Venue

Join us at a landmark of luxury and convenience in the heart of Doha.

Sheraton Grand Doha Resort & Convention Hotel

  • Central Location: Situated on the picturesque Corniche, offering stunning views of the Arabian Gulf.
  • World-Class Facilities: State-of-the-art exhibition halls, meeting rooms, and amenities.
  • Accessibility: Easily accessible from Hamad International Airport and major city attractions.
  • Accommodation: On-site luxury rooms and suites available for a comfortable stay.
Visit Hotel Website

Travel & Accommodation

Visa Information

Citizens of over 80 countries can enter Qatar visa-free. We recommend checking the official Visit Qatar website for the latest visa requirements applicable to your nationality.

Hotel Discounts

We have partnered with several hotels near the venue to offer exclusive discounted rates for QELE 2026 attendees. A list of partner hotels and booking links will be sent to registered attendees closer to the event date.

Explore Doha

Make the most of your trip by exploring Doha's vibrant culture. Visit the Souq Waqif, the Museum of Islamic Art, or the stunning National Museum of Qatar. Doha offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity.
